Statistics show that , in spite of the growing public and private investment in the U.S. child care system , about one-half of employed mother paying for child care change their provider each year . The reason include high cost , hour or location are inconvenient , an unhappy child or parent dislike of a caregiver , or the caregiver resigns . The breakdown of child care arrangement is expensive for employee and it is responsible for about 15 percent in lost work time . Disruption often occurs without notice . As more woman enter the work force , there are fewer relative available to act a a replacement .
