With ever more two-paycheck couple , nanny have become a hot commodity . More than 500 placement agency have sprung up nation-wide . Many do a conscientious job , but some dissatisfied customer tell of unchecked reference , deficient screening , broken contract and unrefunded fee . Many nanny are recruited from rural area in the West or Midwest and have little formal training . Matching nanny to family is a delicate process and some nanny have problem adjusting to the city . Parents warn `` consumer beware '' , while agency point out that some family are problem . A nanny advocacy group is pushing for national standard and training credential for caregiver .
