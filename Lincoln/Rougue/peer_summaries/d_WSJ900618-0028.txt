Many child-care center are becoming surrogate extended family to harried working parent . Some facility provide child-care any time of day or night . Some offer gym and swimming class , and transportation to baseball and soccer game . Many elaborate child-care center are making money in an industry with tight profit margin . Corporations commonly subsidize off-site space for day care that are within 10 to 20 minute away and pay upwards of 30 % of the cost of tuition for their employee . For example , Sunrise Preschools , Inc. run a 24 hour , seven-day-a week center to accommodate America West Airlines flight attendant , baggage handler and other employee with diverse work schedule .
