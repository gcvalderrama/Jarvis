With more two-paycheck couple around , Nannies have become a hot commodity . 500 agency exist nationally to supply them for fee of $ 400- $ 2500 . Most nanny are young woman with child-care experience but little formal training . Screening involves getting childcare and character reference and checking background . Weekly salary range from $ 150- $ 450 or more plus room and board , and sometimes health insurance , paid vacation and holiday , and use of a car . Problems sometimes arise resulting from inadequate screening , lack of compatibility , or dispute over agency fee . The International Nanny Association is pushing for national standard and training credential .
