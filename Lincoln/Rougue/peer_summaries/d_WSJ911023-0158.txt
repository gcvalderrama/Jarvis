Paid child-care leave ha not been extended equally among worker in some company especially if the person requesting the leave happens to be a man . In some case when both men and woman have been denied the same benefit received by some woman in the same office , they have chosen to resign and seek employment elsewhere . Corporate attorney warn that employer are failing to establish policy protecting themselves from the pitfall of giving benefit to the few . A Pittsburgh court ruled against a company that refused a male employee the same parental leave that wa offered to a woman . The Equal Opportunity commission cited that case when it issued new guideline stating that employer must offer the same leave to both men and woman .
