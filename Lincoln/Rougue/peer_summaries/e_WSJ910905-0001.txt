Firms offering their employee child care benefit solve a difficult problem for parent . For the small firm , however , it can be a frantic exercise in the manipulation of scant resource . Benefits include subsidy , referral , flexible benefit program , and dependent care assistance plan . Small company do not attract enough child to prevent the care center from running a deficit , forcing center to close . Small firm must also limit their liability for child safety . Many company owner , however , have an interest in keeping such center viable when they enroll their own child . They believe the center are good for business and help morale .
