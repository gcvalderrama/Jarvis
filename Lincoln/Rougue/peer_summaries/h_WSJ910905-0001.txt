Some small company , in spite of limited resource , are offering employee childcare benefit such a referral service , subsidy , or on-site daycare center . Companies offering benefit are often owned by parent with childcare need of their own , or employ a high percentage of woman . Offering childcare benefit is good for a business 's image , plus morale and productivity go up , hiring is easier , maternity leaf are shorter , and turnover drop a much a 50 % . Problems include administrative hassle that sap a small staff and complaint of inequitable benefit by non-parents . Small company must also be careful to limit liability .
