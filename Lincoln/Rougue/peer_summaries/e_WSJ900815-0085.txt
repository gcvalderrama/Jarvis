A recent 1977-1987 Census Bureau survey report major trend in child care in the U.S. and indicates concern about the affordability of child care by the poor . The use of relative a day care provider declined , probably reflecting in part the greater number of female relative of poorer family who entered the work force . According to the survey , poor family spend about one fourth of their income on probably le quality child care . This compare with one sixth spent by higher income family . The report could support current congressional legislation to provide financial assistance to low income family .
