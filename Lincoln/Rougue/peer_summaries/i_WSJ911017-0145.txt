A growing number of big company are joining joint venture to try creative solution to the nation 's child-care problem , believed to cost U.S. business some $ 3 billion a year . Corporate dollar , aimed at raising the supply and quality of care , are beginning to make a difference . Joint day-care center have been built . Other program are creating new slot for child in family day-care center . Programs also offer incentive for professional training program that focus on health and safety issue and entrepreneurship . Other company have found that even a small increase in employee benefit , such a low-cost health benefit , have a big impact on turnover and credential of child-care worker .
