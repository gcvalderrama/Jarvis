U.S. corporation are joining together in the establishment of child care center . This trend result from productivity loss brought on by the shortage and the high cost of quality child care . Corporate dollar are making the difference . In the new center , child-teacher ratio are one-half the national average , and pay and benefit are above average . The center are well-staffed , and often give peace of mind to company parent . Some corporate partnership have provided a critical solution : establishment of center for the care of sick child . Corporate collaboration may be the best response to chronic child care problem .
