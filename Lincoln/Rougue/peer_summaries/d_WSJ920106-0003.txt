The Internal Revenue Service now allows licensed day-care provider to deduct cost for any portion of their home used for regular day care during any part of the day . No record are required for specific hour of usage and a room doe not have to be used every business day to quality . IRS Ruling 923 is a new position since last April when the IRS had a dispute with one woman . They wanted her to account for each hour of use of each room in her home . Now a provider can count any room used for a portion of the day a long a they are regularly used and available for day care .
