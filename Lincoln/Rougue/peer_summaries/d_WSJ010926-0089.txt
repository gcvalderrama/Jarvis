Child-care arrangement remain unreliable despite growing public and private investment in child-care system . About one-half of employed mother who pay for child care change care giver each year . About 345 out of 2,300 woman surveyed reported lost time from work in the past month . The problem cost employer about $ 3 billion annually . Working mother can no longer depend on relative to provide day-care on a regular or emergency basis . More and more woman have taken other job and are not available to care for young family member . Other day-care worker often quit with little or no notice leaving mother with no alternative but to take time off from work until they can make new arrangement .
