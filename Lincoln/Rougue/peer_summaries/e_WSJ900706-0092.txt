In the search for child care , turning to a high profile agency is no guarantee of reliable service . It may result in a lawsuit for poor or negligent service . The increasing number of two-paycheck parent searching for nanny ha resulted in a proliferation of child care agency . Most do a good job . For those that do not , complaint include unchecked reference , deficient screening , broken contract and unrefunded fee . Complaints against nanny include poor training and child abuse . In an attempt to prevent abuse within this burgeoning industry , some organization are establishing national standard and training credential for nanny .
