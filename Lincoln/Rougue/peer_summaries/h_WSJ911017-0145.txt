Worried that a shortage of high-quality childcare is hurting productivity , a growing number of company are forming joint venture to stretch benefit dollar . Creative solution include subsidizing facility and effort to win accreditation , offering childcare worker incentive to get professional training and health benefit to improve retention and quality , promoting new home-based center , and building center of their own . The biggest collaboration so far is a two million dollar center in Charlotte , NC , financed by IBM and four partner , open to sponsor ' employee and the public . Allstate Insurance published a guidebook to help manager seek out childcare collaborator .
