Many child-care center are becoming surrogate extended family to increase appeal . They are becoming more flexible , including 24-7 availability and convenient location , and offering novel service such a vacationer/conventioneer support , recreational class , transportation service , and take-home meal . At the same time they have developed sophisticated marketing strategy to actually make money in an industry with a tight profit margin . Their target client are affluent , two-income family and corporation . Parents need more than traditional day care and some are willing to pay for it and employer are willing to subsidize it . Successful company include Sunrise Preschools Inc. and Lakeshore Teaching and Learning Center .
