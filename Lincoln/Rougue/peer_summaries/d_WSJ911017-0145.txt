In order to make high-quality day care affordable , many large corporation are joining together to stretch their child-care dollar . The high cost to corporation to set up quality day care for their employee have caused some corporation to work together . They are pooling fund to provide center with more teacher per child allowing specialized care . The increase in teacher also provides a calmer environment that is reflected in better behavior of the child . Joint effort allow teacher to be paid better and receive benefit such a health care . This tends to decrease the turn over of child care provider and increase the quality of applicant .
