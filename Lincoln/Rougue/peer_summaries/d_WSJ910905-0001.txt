Small company that try to provide day-care center for their employee often run into financial problem . Generally , there may not be enough child to support the cost of running a center . Some company are solving this problem by opening up the center to the public . In other case , small company-run day care center have been good for business . They say that hiring is easier , maternity leaf are shorter and turnover is off by a much a 50 % . Still , small business face a tougher time than big business in offering child-care benefit . One problem is caused by not having enough employee to handle administrative hassle such a meeting zoning and building-code regulation .
