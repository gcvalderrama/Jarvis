Dateline August 9, 1990.  Yesterday Iraq announced that it formally
annexed Kuwait, the neighboring, oil-producing nation it invaded last
Thursday.  The U.S. and Iraq appear closer to confrontation over the
situation.  Referring to the buildup of U.S. forces in the Region,
Iraq said they would fight.  America's President Bush stressed that
our force buildup is intended to defend the threatened Saudi kingdom.

Iraq fields a battle-trained army wielding modern Soviet-made tanks
and formidable air- defense weapons.  It's surface-to-surface Scud
missiles and chemical arsenal is daunting.  However, General Powell
said, "We view them as a capable military force.  But they are not
invincible...."
