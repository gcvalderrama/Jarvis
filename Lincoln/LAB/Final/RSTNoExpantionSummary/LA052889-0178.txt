Capt. Will C. Rogers III was awarded the Legion of Merit , a top military honor , on Saturday 
as he stepped down from command of the Vincennes 
Rogers was given the award for his performance as commanding officer of the guided-missile cruiser , 
which became the focus of international controversy last July 
when it mistakenly shot down an Iranian airliner in the Persian Gulf , killing all 290 people aboard 
He has been reassigned to shore duty at the Point Loma Tactical Development and Training Group in what Navy officials describe as a routine transfer
