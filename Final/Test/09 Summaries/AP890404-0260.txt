Tuesday, Exxon said it had finished transferring about 42 million
gallons of crude from the grounded Exxon Valdez.  Crews will attempt
to refloat the tanker on Wednesday.  After a brief stop for repairs,
Exxon intends to take the ship to dry dock in the Far East or
Portland, Oregon.  The captain, accused of operating a ship under the
influence of alcohol, among other things, wants to surrender.  On
Monday, Alaska canceled the herring season for this year.  Other
fisheries in Prince William waters are also closed.  Efforts to save a
salmon hatchery appear to be failing.  Gas prices rose sharply in
California.
