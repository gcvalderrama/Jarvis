A USAF F-111 fighter-bomber, attached to the 48th Tactical Fighter
Wing based at RAF Lakenheath Air Base in Britain, crashed at dawn
today in southern Saudi Arabia while on a training mission, killing
both crew members. It was the fourth US aircraft deployed for
Operation Desert Shield to crash in 3 days.

Eight marines are missing in the crash of two UH-1 Huey helicopters in
the northern Arabian Sea on Monday.

A USAF F-4 reconnaissance jet also went down Monday, killing both crew
members.

13 other USAF personnel were killed in the crash of a C-5 jet cargo
plane in Germany.
