A US F-15 fighter belonging to the 44th Fighter Squadron of the 18th
Wing crashed on take-off from the Kadena Air Base on Okinawa at 0924
on 4 April. The fighter was on a routine training mission and the
crash occurred within the base's facilities, about 800 meters
northeast of a runway. The pilot ejected safely. After the crash,
Japan's Defense Facilities Administration Agency asked the command of
the US Forces in Japan to take thorough measures for safe management
of the US military bases on Okinawa and conduct a thorough
investigation into the cause of the crash.
