Ernesto Beatriz Martinez of Mexico won the San Diego International
Marathon on Sunday.  His time was 2:16:12, 39 seconds ahead of South
African Mark Plaatjes.  Marie Rollins, from Ireland, won the women's
race in 2:39:05 with Mindy Ireland of Escondido, California, second.
The winners each received $5,000.  Doug Kurtis of Detroit ran his 12th
marathon in a calendar year under 2:20, finishing fourth.  Maurilio
Castillo was awarded third place, although he had originally entered
the accompanying half marathon and mistakenly followed the longer
route.  Bill Fricke of Los Angeles won the wheelchair marathon in
1:55:30.
