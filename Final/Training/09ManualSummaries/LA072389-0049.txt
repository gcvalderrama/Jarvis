Las Vegas hotel construction is booming as never before.  1992 will
add more than 20,000 hotel rooms according to hotel owners and
industry analyst.

The $630-million Mirage will have 3,100 rooms and the $290-million
Excalibur will have 4,032.  These two hotels will be the first major
projects in the city in 15 years.  Saul Leonard, a gaming industry
expert said "Las Vegas is the third largest Convention City in the
United States and probably will be No. 2 within the next two years."
