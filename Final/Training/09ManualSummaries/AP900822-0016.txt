A chronology of the crisis: From August 1 through 5, Iraq threatened,
invaded then consolidated its hold on Kuwait.  August 6 through 9, the
United Nations orders a trade embargo against Iraq; the U.S. begins a
regional military buildup; Iraq seals its borders.  August 10 through
13, Arab countries join in the defense of Saudi Arabia.  Saddam seeks
Arab support by involving Israel in any possible settlement.  August
14 through 16, King Hussein of Jordan fails in his mediation efforts.
August 17 through 21, the U.S. increases its buildup and embargo
efforts.  Iraq uses hostages as shields at military
installations.
