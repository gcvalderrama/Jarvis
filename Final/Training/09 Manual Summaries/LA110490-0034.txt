What's On in Las Vegas is a handy biweekly magazine the lists
entertainment and others attraction around the city. It can be useful
to senior citizens who are looking for special discounts.

A regular feature, "What's On for the Young at Heart" gives such
information as where to find discounted hotel rooms, food and
cosmetology services.  A special bargain featured by the transit
system gives senior with a valid Medicare card a 10-ride pass for
$4.20, which would normally cost $11.00.

The magazine is available in most hotels and also informs readers of
discounts on rental cars, motor scooters, and wheelchairs.
